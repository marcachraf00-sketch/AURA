What is Aura?

Aura is your live, real-world experience ranking system synced natively via secure, physical NFC hardware identity cards.

By bridging the gap between hardware items and cloud profile states, Aura turns physical interactions into direct account progression.

⚡ Instant NFC Syncing Tap your mobile device directly to a physical Aura card to process profile triggers instantly.
📈 Level & XP Engines Earn $+5\text{ XP}$ bonuses on real-world interactions to scale your rank and level up.
🔒 Secure Handshakes Built on top of secure database architecture to protect individual profile records.
